Calculate the Universe Calculator

Calculate the Universe is an in-game skill that, when cast, takes an input value and gives different results, depending on a few different factors. This page can determine what input number to use.

"Ascensions" is the number listed on your character page.

If you use mafia, "numberology 69" in the graphical CLI will give you +3 adventures. Or "numberology 37" for +3 pvp fights, etc.
